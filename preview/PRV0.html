Our Story

Twelve years
of slow bread.

001 — Provenance
Milled in Kildare
We work with Danaher's stone mill. Irish grain, cold-pressed, full of character. You taste the difference in the first bite.
002 — Community
Bread for Dublin Simon
Every evening, day-old loaves go to Dublin Simon Community. Good food, shared fairly.
003 — Waste
Near-zero kitchen
Compostable packaging, no single-use plastics, and spent grain goes to a local smallholding.
"Good bread needs patience — a virtue Dublin has always known."

— Siobhán Ó'Brien, Head Baker & Founder
